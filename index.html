About Atula International

Atula International is a premier global trading house, recognized as a pioneer in the import and export of high-quality industrial minerals, precision-engineered stones, and luxury natural products. With decades of operational excellence, we have bridged the gap between raw potential and refined beauty.

Our foundation is built on unshakeable pillars of integrity, global connectivity, and customer-centric innovation. Headquartered in a strategic trade hub, Atula International leverages state-of-the-art logistics and a vast supplier network to ensure that every product delivered meets the highest standards of durability and finesse.
